About RateMyCustomers.com

RateMyCustomers.com is a restricted-access business intelligence platform designed to assist verified businesses in evaluating customer-related risk prior to entering into service agreements.

Purpose of the System

The platform exists to provide small and medium-sized businesses with a structured mechanism for documenting and sharing customer experiences that may not be visible through traditional consumer review platforms.

While customers are able to publicly review businesses, there has historically been no equivalent system allowing businesses to evaluate customer behavior patterns. This platform addresses that imbalance.

Types of Information Reported

Participating businesses may submit reports including, but not limited to:

  • Non-payment, late payment, or disputed invoices
  • Chargebacks, cancellations, or refund abuse
  • Repeated disputes or service interruptions
  • Misrepresentation of facts during transactions
  • Public review activity considered harmful or misleading

How the System Works

Verified businesses submit structured reports based on direct interactions with customers. These reports are then made available within a closed network of approved business users.

Each submission remains under the control of the originating business. RateMyCustomers.com does not independently modify, interpret, or remove submitted content unless explicitly requested by the submitting business or when stated conditions for resolution are met.

Business Use and Decisioning

Businesses may use available information to make internal risk assessments. Depending on the nature of a report, a business may choose to:

  • Decline service engagement
  • Adjust pricing or deposit requirements
  • Require additional verification prior to service

Dispute Handling

RateMyCustomers.com does not mediate disputes between parties. Any resolution must be handled directly between the customer and the reporting business.

In many cases, review status changes are contingent on resolution terms defined by the submitting business, which may include corrective actions or mutual agreement between both parties.

Access Restrictions

Access to the platform is strictly limited to verified business entities. Unauthorized access or misuse of system data is prohibited.
